About Us

Welcome to Homescaped

your dedicated partner in nurturing and maintaining the beauty and serenity of your home garden and property. Based in the heart of Sydney's Northen Beaches and extending our warm services to the vibrant North Shore, we are a team of passionate horticulturists, arborists, and property maintenance experts committed to bringing your outdoor vision to life.


We believe that every garden tells a story

and every property reflects its owner's pride. That's why we offer bespoke solutions tailored to the unique landscape and lifestyle needs of our clients. From the lush, green expanses of sprawling estates to the quaint, cozy corners of suburban retreats, our hands-on approach ensures that every blade of grass, every hedge trimming and every paving stone is perfect.
