Welcome to Homescaped,
Where Nature Meets Nurture

At Homescaped, we believe in turning houses into homes and gardens into sanctuaries. As a family-run business, our roots in landscaping and home property styling  & maintenance run as deep as the trees we plant. Since our humble beginnings in 1975, Homescaped has been more than just a business to us; it's a legacy of love for nature and nurturing spaces.

Our founder, a visionary with a green thumb and a heart full of dreams, laid the foundation of Homescaped on the principles of quality, care, and creativity. Now, as we gracefully pass the baton to the next generation, our commitment to transforming your outdoor spaces into breathtaking landscapes remains unchanged.

Your home,
Our passion

With the touch of tradition and a modern twist, we continue to bring the same dedication and attention to detail that has been our hallmark for over four decades. At Homescaped, every plant we choose, every design we conceptualize, and every garden we bring to life, is a reflection of our family's passion for beauty and functionality.
